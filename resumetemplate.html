Can I customize the templates?

Yes! All templates are built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, making them fully customizable. You can modify colors, fonts, layouts, and add your own styling to match your personal brand.

What technical skills do I need?

Basic knowledge of HTML and CSS is helpful but not required. Our templates come with clear documentation and are designed to be user-friendly for beginners while offering advanced customization options for developers.

What file formats are included?

Each template package includes HTML files, CSS stylesheets, JavaScript files, and all necessary assets. You'll also receive a PDF export feature and print-optimized styles for physical copies.

Can I use these for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! Once purchased, you have full commercial usage rights. Use the templates for personal job applications, client work, or even resell customized versions as part of your services.

Do you provide support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and email support to help you get the most out of your templates. Our community forum is also available for peer assistance.
